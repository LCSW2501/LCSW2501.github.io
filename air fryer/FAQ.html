Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I clean my air fryer?

Use warm soapy water and a soft cloth. Avoid using metal utensils.

2. Do your air fryers come with a warranty?

Yes, all our air fryers come with a 1-year warranty.

3. Can I return a product?

Yes, within 30 days of purchase with proof of receipt.

4. Are the parts dishwasher safe?

Most removable parts are dishwasher safe. Check the manual for specifics.
